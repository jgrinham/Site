Spaghetti on a Stick

by Jonathan Grinham, D.Des

Find Out More

From Paper Robots to Thermal Monocoques

Jonathan is a postdoctoral fellow at the Harvard Center for Green Buildings and Cities. He received his doctorate degree at the Harvard University Graduate School of Design where his research developed fabrication and design methods for milli- to micro-scale flow systems for thermal control in buildings. His past research agendas track themes in interactive and responsive environments, embedded computation, computational design, and automated fabrication workflows.
